DISCOGRAPHY

Albums and Cassette

[ATEEZ FEVER] Super global rookie idol ATEEZ is back with its second album, following the narrative of the previous soryline. Titled as the "Pirate Kings of Kpop", they have really got a grip on the hallyu wave. The album charted on #1 on Hanteo Album Charts, got 3 music show wins. The teaser for the album was released on 29th August, 2020 on Jinhit's official youtube channel which garnered over 10 million views within one day and the sales exceeded 300,000 on the first week.

[ATEEZ TREASURE: EPILOGUE] The treasure:epilogue marks the beginning of the journey of 7 pirate boys who dream of being young forever and so, are trying to reach their destination: neverland. However, because of a certain someone, the time machine brings them to the past when they were still strangers to each other. Who rewinded the time machine? But more importantly, will they meet each other once again? and if they do, what story'll unfold?

[MAMAMOO WINDFLOWER] MAMAMOO has always been breaking the norms of a typical kpop group and experimenting with new sounds and music. They have been the icon of "HIP" ever since they debuted but this album is a bit different. Packed with nostalgic melodies and strong vocals, the girls have something new to bring onto the plate. The nostalgic memory of the first love, the big dreams which once seemed easy to achieve, the feeling of the first heartbreak; the album "windflower" has so much to say.

[SEVENTEEN HENGARAE] 7years onto their debut, SEVENTEEN has matured throughout their phase in the industry. Hengarae album is a push to the modern youngsters to not lose sight of their dream and continue to strive to achieve their dreams; no matter big or small. The members themselves have also taken part not only in producing and composing the songs but also, the choreography. No wonder, they are called the self-producing idols. Infact they have outgrown the title of idols and are considered true artists.

[THE ROSE DAWN] Dawn is an appeal to the 21st gen youngsters to be more aware of the surroundings. The dog you bought because you wanted a pet, the paintbrushes you bought because you were inspired by an artist, the cup you got as a gift, where are they now? Sometimes, we take small things like those for granted. The band, ROSE is not afraid to share their stories through the slice-of-life kind of lyrics that their fans can relate to. Due to their large international fanbase, they performed at the Wembley Stadium in 2019 in London.

[TWICE FANCY] "Nation's girl group" Twice truly have lived up to their names. Fancy album charted at #1 on the Orican Charts of Japan, which is the first time for a non-Japanese album. They also outsold the Tokyo Dome on May this year. Moreover, Fancy became the highest selling album of 2020 breaking the record of SEVENTEEN which was 1.2 million physical sales. THe girls have showed their potential eversince their debut with a loyal fanbase and amazing sale records.

[TXT STAR] "The star is their parent, they'll go where he leads them." They lived happily together in the magic island but the star slept one day and never woke up again. The five boys lost their way but fate is bringing them together. However, everytime they go to the magic island, one of them dies. The loop never ends. The loop is supposed to continue until they realize they are the children of the star. Will they realize it? Or will they be in this loop forever?

[TXT MAGIC] The young teenage boys have captured hearts of a lot of fans in their 3 months of promotion. Following the narration of their debut album, their second album, TXT MAGIC is on the darker side of the previous album where they talk about the temptation of the dark magic, which is inspired by the book "Demian". TXT MAGIC album surpassed 200,000 million copies on its first month of release and their debut music video garnered 5.6 million views within 24 hours on Jinhit's official youtube channel.
